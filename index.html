How does Neo Rx work?

assessment of communication, social interaction, play and behaviors in children.

    Give personal and skill-related information about your child

  • Assessment

    Free assessment for the child based on VBMAPP to create personalized learning module

  • Learning

    Customized therapy module for the child covering social, verbal, behavioral and academic skill domains.

  • Progress Report

    AI generated personalized report showing learning path and growth of child

The Expert Speaks

Neo-Rx has been created to provide supplementary special education for children with autism spectrum disorder and other developmental delays. Children require high quality services for an extended time to have a positive effect on their life.

Our goal is to provide children with skills that will lead them to being placed in the least restrictive setting possible and to increase their independence. The application was created based on the science of applied behavior analysis, and it uses evidence-based procedures to maximize learning for the child.
